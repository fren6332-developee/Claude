Frequently asked questions

Where is my information stored?

Only on your device, in the browser/app's local storage. Nothing is uploaded. If you switch devices or clear your data, it will be gone unless you exported a backup first (Backup & print → Export backup).

How do I move my data to a new phone?

On the old device: Backup & print → Export backup. Save the file (email it to yourself or use a drive). On the new device: open the app → Backup & print → Restore from backup and pick the file.

The "Listen" button doesn't speak.

Audio uses your device's built-in text-to-speech. Make sure your volume is up and the device isn't on silent. It works best in Safari (iOS) and Chrome. On some devices you may need to tap once to allow audio.

Can I print a summary for my doctor?

Yes — Backup & print → Open printable summary. It gathers the diagnosis, vitals, history, and the questions you've checked. Use your device's Print → "Save as PDF" to keep or share it.

Is this medical advice?

No. It is an educational and organizing tool. It cannot diagnose or recommend treatment. Always talk to your licensed care team before making decisions.

How do I delete everything?

Backup & print → Erase all data on this device. This cannot be undone, so export a backup first if you want to keep anything.
