Privacy Policy

Scott M.'s Prostate Health Guide · Last updated July 17, 2026

The short version: This app stores everything you enter only on your own device. There is no account, no server, and no tracking. We never see, collect, transmit, or sell your data.

What the app stores

The app lets you save health information — a name, diagnosis and pathology text, Gleason/ISUP/stage, vitals, a PSA log, health history, and medical images you upload. All of this is written to your device's local browser storage (localStorage and IndexedDB). It stays on that device.

What we collect

Nothing. The app has no back-end server, no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party trackers. Your health information is never uploaded anywhere by the app.

Backups and printing

If you use the Export backup feature, the app creates a file on your device that you control. If you use the Printable summary, it opens a page you can print or save. Where those files go afterward is up to you — keep them somewhere secure.

External links

The app links to public resources (for example ClinicalTrials.gov, PubMed, the National Cancer Institute). Opening a link takes you to that third party's website, which has its own privacy policy. The app does not send your data to them.

Audio

Spoken explanations use your device's built-in text-to-speech. The text is read locally; no audio or text is sent to us or to any server.

Children

This app is intended for adults managing health information. It is not directed at children.

Deleting your data

You can erase everything from within the app (Backup & print → "Erase all data on this device"), or by clearing the browser/app data on your device.

Not medical advice

This app is an educational and organizing tool. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult a licensed healthcare professional.
